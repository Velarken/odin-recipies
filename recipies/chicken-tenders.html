Chicken Tender Recipe

Description of the recipe

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1.25 cups panko
  • Garlic and onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper
  • 4 boneless chicken tenders

Steps

  1. Make breading
  2. Dredge chicken
  3. Bake chicken tenders
