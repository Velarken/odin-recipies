Pizza Recipie

Description of recipe here


Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oik
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Mix yeast and sugar with warm water
    according to the package
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture
    beat until smooth. You can do this
    by hand or with a mixer
  4. Let rest for 5-10 minutes
  5. Shape and roll out dough, leaving a slight crust
  6. Add sauce, cheese, seasonings, and any
    other ingredients that you may like
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until golden brown
  8. Let cool 2-3 minutes before cutting and serving
