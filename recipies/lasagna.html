Learn how to make a delicious lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage
  • Ground beef
  • 1 whole Onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses of your choice
  • Egg if using ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
